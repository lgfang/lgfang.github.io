This feature is overlooked by many. And it was the case in my team as well. We used to create voting webpages to collect votes (we are a bunch of software enigneers anyway). But, now we send out voting emails instead as it is much simpler.

  • To create and send out a poll, just "Use Voting Buttons" (customize options per your needs).

  • Upon receiving this kind of emails, you can
    • Either respond in the preview pane directly

    • Or click the "Vote" button after openning the email.

For more details, refer to MS office document.
