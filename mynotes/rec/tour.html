D1: [2022-04-09 Sat] Sydney -> Canberra -> Lakes Entrance ~ 720km/10hr

  1. [2022-04-09 Sat 07:30]–[2022-04-09 Sat 11:30] Sydney -> Canberra ~ 290km/4hr
    • 导航至堪培拉唐人街 KFC 81 Woolley St, Dickson ACT 2602；沿途每两小时休 息一次，总共休息两、三次。
    • 最后一次短暂休息建议设在 Weereewa Lookout：可以欣赏Lake George 湖 景。丰水时是湖，枯水时变草原，远处有风力电场。 https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_George_(New_South_Wales)
    • Hume highway 在 Sutton 和 Goulburn 之间属于 Coast to the Highlands scenic drive 的一部分。
  2. [2022-04-09 Sat 11:30]–[2022-04-09 Sat 13:00] OPTIONAL 简单午餐+休息
    • 就近找中餐馆或快餐店吃饭
  3. [2022-04-09 Sat 13:00]–[2022-04-09 Sat 19:00] Canberra -> Lakes Entrance ~ 430km/6hr

    • NOTE 无法在18:00前赶到的话，务必在下班前电话联系前台

      Check-in is from 2pm through to 6pm. Arrival after this time is available by contacting reception during office hours.

  4. [2022-04-09 Sat 19:00] 入住、晚餐
    • 家里或半路（堪培拉）带食物
    • 快餐：鱼薯、麦当劳（网红店 Wild Catch Fish and Chips 营业时间有限；有 机会可以试试。我们感觉味道一般。）

D2: [2022-04-10 Sun] Lakes Entrance https://www.lakesentrance.com

  • 抓螃蟹 https://www.lakesentrance.com/explore/crabbing
    • 工具：螃蟹笼、鸡架（官网推荐）、长绳子（15m+，把笼子放到底）、烧烤用 大夹子（夹螃蟹）、冰桶。
      • 注意：VIC 和 NSW 规定不一样。确保买的蟹笼符合 VIC 的规定。
    • 此处全年都允许抓螃蟹，最佳时间10月至来年1月。但网友复活节假期也大丰收。
    • 渔证不用提前准备，去抓螃蟹的路上去 Lakes Entrance Visitor Centre 现买就 行。
    • 官方推荐地点 Reeve Landing Jetty。对此网友众说纷纭，有说抓不到的，有说 很容易抓的。但网友一致肯定租船可以丰收，新足迹网友 在 Kalimna Jetty Walk 所对湖面收获颇丰。租船店家也说在那里抓螃蟹。
    • 潮汐：专家似乎认为最佳时间 slack tide (in many places one hour either side of high or low tide)，但去 Lakes Entrance 的经验好像什么时候都行。
    • 网友说很多住处不让煮螃蟹。但可以确定 Big4 Whiters Holiday Village 不禁 止房客煮螃蟹（它自己网站就有CRABBING IN LAKES ENTRENACE）。
  • 租船
    • 不需要 Boat License。
    • 抓螃蟹、看风景一举两得。
    • Vistor Center 门口沿湖的路上就好几家，我们用的是 Portside Boat Hire (260/274 Marine Parade, Lakes Entrance VIC 3909)。
    • 我们4大人3小孩租了一艘8人船3小时，费用$180。感觉再来一小时也可以:)。
  • 直升机观景
    • https://lakesentrancehelicopters.com.au/product-category/scenics/
    • 机场距 Lakes Entrance 15分钟车程，空中飞行时间9-42分钟；推荐20分钟 $200。

D3: [2022-04-11 Mon] Lakes Entrance -> Philip Island ~ 300km/4hr

  1. [2022-04-11 Mon 09:00]–[2022-04-11 Mon 09:30] 酒店退房
  2. [2022-04-11 Mon 09:30]–[2022-04-11 Mon 13:30] 在途
  3. [2022-04-11 Mon 13:30]–[2022-04-11 Mon 16:30] 入住+自由活动
    • 参观巧克力工厂
    • 买企鹅票时可以考虑买四个公园的联票
    • 公园、湿地 https://www.penguins.org.au/attractions/reserves/
      • Wetlands of Rhyll Inlet : 湿地里有很长的 boardwalk，可以骑车边走 边看
  4. [2022-04-11 Mon 16:30] 看企鹅归巢 Penguin Parade :
    • NOTE 切记在购票官网查询开始时间，不同等级的票，开始时间也不一样。 [2022-04-11 Mon 17:45] General Viewing 开始，停车场到看台约20分钟 路程。早点去占位：前排、沙滩、boardwalk。
    • NOTE 海边晚上低温大风，多加衣服。
    • 买票: https://www.penguins.org.au/buy-tickets/

      • General Viewing vs Penguin Plus: 有人认为 General Viewing 就挺好（如 下）但我强烈建议 Penguin Plus。我们 General Viewing 的体验是企鹅三三 两两地上岸；而且远处企鹅成群结队非常壮观地上岸 Parade 。我们猜测那 是 Penguin Plus 处。

        I just wanted to add to this thread for anyone's future reference, but I have done both the penguin plus and general admission experiences and agree that general admission can get very busy, however, after the first penguins come up the beach, the crowd of people begins to leave the stands and walk back up the boardwalks, and patience absolutely pays off as many more penguins will keep arriving at the beach for some time, giving ample opportunities for viewing them in this seating area. It is well worth letting the crowd begin to leave and moving to get a better view if you are seated too far away. The reward for this is often the penguins begin to arrive in bigger numbers as the night gets darker, you can then take a leisurely walk back to the visitor centre watching the penguins arrive in their burrows. The penguin plus experience is a little quieter, with slightly higher viewing seats, an interesting talk from a ranger before the penguins arrive and an audio guide to listen to while you wait, which is very informative and well worth the extra money if you would prefer less of the crowds. I believe both experiences can be wonderful.

    • 新足迹帖子： https://www.oursteps.com.au/bbs/forum.php?mod=redirect&goto=findpost&ptid=1175657&pid=25171611
