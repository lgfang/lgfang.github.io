澳大利亚

NSW内陆四日游 - Dubbo + Narrabri

  • 特点：动物园、Mt Kaputar National Park、射电望远镜阵列、Dark Sky Park
  • 注意：
    • 四天三晚
    • 距离远（距悉尼5、6个小时车程）
  • 行程：
    1. D1: 经 Orange 前往 Dubbo - 车程约5.5小时
      1. 8 am 出发前往 Bathurst Visitor Infromation Centre会合 - 车程约2.75小时
        • Bathurst 超市、快餐店、加油站一应俱全。
        • Bathurst 也有一些景点(Attractions & Tours)，有时间的话可以考虑访问。
        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • Richmond Shopping Mall
          • North Richmond public toilets
          • Lithgow Caltex
          • Bathurst BP etc.
      2. 继续驾车 Dubbo - 车程约2.75小时
        • Dubbo 同样超市、快餐店、加油站一应俱全。
        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • [ ] Mitchell Highway rest area
          • [X] North Orange Shopping Centre: 超市、快餐店、加油站俱全，可以 在此歇脚+加油+午餐
          • [ ] Toongi public toilets
      3. 游览 Royal Flying Doctor Service Visitor Experience
      4. 入住酒店
    2. D2: 游览动物园 + 前往 Narrabri - 车程约4小时
      1. 酒店退房
      2. 9 am - 4 pm 游览动物园
        • 动物园
        • 很大，玩一、两天都可以
        • 门票连续两天有效
        • 需要开车进去或租自行车(全天不到$20)
      3. 驾车至 Sliding Spring Observatory 小憩 - 车程约2小时
        • Coonabarabran is the stargazing capital of Australia.
          • Warrumbungle National Park: the first International Dark Sky Park in Australia.
          • Sliding Spring Observatory + Worlds Largest Virtual Solar System Drive

        • 可以考虑在此住宿一晚，夜访天文台观星:

          For stargazing through powerful telescopes visit the Milroy Observatory or the Warrumbungle Observatory. Both observatories offer experts to guide you through the stars. You can even stay at the Skywatch Observatory, which has a large telescope.

        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • Coo-Ee March Memorial Park
          • Tooraweenah Rest Area
      4. 继续驾车往 Narrabri 入住酒店 - 车程约2小时
        • Narrabri 超市、快餐店一应俱全。
        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • Coonabarabran 镇上有超市、加油站
          • 沿途若干简易休息区
          • Narrabri Public Toilet / Rest area
      5. 晚餐
    3. D3: Narrabri 周边游玩 - 车程约（3.5+4）小时
      1. 驾车前往 Australia Telescope Compact Array (ATCA) - 车程约0.5小时
        • The visitors centre is open from 8am to 5pm daily … staying overnight is strictly prohibited. We do however host photographers in the evening roughly once per month while the Milky Way is visible and the Moon does not wash out the stars.
        • Yarrie Lake, a 1.5km-diameter circular lake, is located about 10km south-west from the observatory, and is well worth a visit. It is a beautiful spot for a picnic.

      2. 驾车原路返回然后前往 Doug Sky Lookout，West Kaputar Lookout 和 Mt Kaputar Summit - 车程约1小时 Note

        Most of Mount Kaputar National Park is now open. However, the following areas are closed due to bushfire damage and will remain closed until it is safe to open them:

        • Mount Kaputar Summit walk
        • West Kaputar lookout
      3. 驾车前往 Sawn Rocks - 车程约1.5小时
        • 停车场前往 Sawn Rocks 为约700米铺装道路，可走轮椅
      4. 返回酒店 - 车程约0.5小时
      5. 晚餐

      6. Optional: 若夜空晴朗考虑驾车前往 Sliding Spring Observatory - 车程往 返约3.5小时

        • 夜空晴朗且新月前后适合拍摄星空。满月前后月亮太亮，盖过星空。考虑上 半夜日落、月升或凌晨月落、日升之间访问或寄希望于云彩挡住月亮。
          • TODEL: 2021-01-25
            • Sunset 8:08 pm, Last Light 8:34 pm
            • Moon rise 5:18 pm, Meridian Passing 10:27 pm
          • TODEL: 2021-01-26
            • Sun: First Light 5:58 am; Sunrise 6:24 am
            • Moon set: 3:33 am

        Note:

        Due to urgent repair work, the Exploratory and Café will be closed until further notice (from Friday 8th Jan). This will mean no access to Siding Spring Observatory unless you have booked a tour.

    4. D4: 经 Quirindi 返回 - 车程约（6.5）小时
      1. 酒店退房
      2. 加满油
      3. 驾车前往 Quirindi 看向日葵 - 车程约2.5小时

        • 导航至 Liverpool Plains Visitor Information Centre

          LATEST NEWS 11 January 2021

          The small crop that one of our local farmers has planted is starting to flower! The best time to come and see it is between 15th January until the end of the month. Remember, it is only a small crop, so don’t expect miles and miles of sunflowers! But you can enter it without contacting the farmer. Please be respectful of the surrounding crops and other visitors. There is a donation box at the crop, it would be great if you showed the farmer your appreciation by contributing!

          Call in to the Visitor Information Centre in Willow Tree for directions!

        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • Boggabri Public Toilets / Rest Area
          • Gunnedah 超市、快餐店、加油站
      4. 驾车返回 - 车程约4小时
        • 沿途休整、补给点：
          • [ ] Muswellbrook: 沿线的 Donald's Muswellbrook 可供休整
          • [ ] Singleton: 沿线的 BP 加油站 24hr
          • [ ] Rixs Creek Rest Area: 入口需下主路几十米
          • [X] Wattaka Rest Area East - Liverpool Plains Visitor Information Centre 至此 2.2 小时
          • [ ] Caltex M1 Southbound: 加油、快餐
